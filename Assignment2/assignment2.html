Oregon must-see's!

Map 1:

Crater Lake National Park, the only national park in Oregon! One of the most beautiful sights in North America, this lake was formed of a caldera from the Mt. Mazama volcano approximately 7700 years ago. Pinned is Wizard Island, a volcanic island that sits inside of the massive lake! Crater lake is the deepest lake in the United States, and the 9th deepest lake in the world. Additionally, during the winter this place completely transforms as the area has some of the heaviest annual snowfall in North America, don't forget to pack those snow shoes!

Map 2:

Honeyman State Park! The closest state park campground near Florence, Oregon. This place features showers, a hiker/biker camp, and a lake! This place should be at the top of your radar for camping along the coast. The facilities are great and clean, while offering many nearby amenities such as dune buggy-ing, hiking, and of course the Pacific Ocean!

Map 3:

Tillamook Creamery is one of the most beautiful places on Earth, and an essential for anyone visiting Oregon. Pounds and pounds of cheese move through this factory every day, and with windows over looking their production floor, you can see the Tillamook employees in action! Not only is this place a factory of dairy production, but it's a full restarurant and shop for local Tillamook goods! The mac and cheese is a delight, and of course the delicious Tillamook ice cream to top it off.
